Detailed Description

starts at 0,0 and explores the whole maze. kmaze is the known maze, and is used to "read the sensors". The mouse solves the maze after sensing each new square. solves assuming ALL walls, and assuming NO walls. when the solution for those two mazes are the same, then it knows the fastest route.
